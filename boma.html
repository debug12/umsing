Best of Michigan A Cappella CD

The Michigan A Cappella Council proudly announces that the "Best of Michigan A Cappella" CD is available for purchase. All proceeds will be going to VH1 Save The Music, a charitable organization that supports Music Education in public schools. We are proud to be able to give back to the community and support school music programs; a cause that is fundamental to the future of collegiate a cappella.
