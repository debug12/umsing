Best of Michigan A Cappella CD to benefit Music Education in Public Schools

The Michigan A Cappella Council proudly announces that the brand new "Best of Michigan A Cappella" CD is now available for purchase. All proceeds will be going to VH1 Save The Music, a charitable organization that supports Music Education in public schools. We are proud to be able to give back to the community and support school music programs; a cause that is fundamental to the future of collegiate a cappella.

We are happy to ship orders to you! Please email us at maccexec@umich.edu to place an order!
